BrightCart Traffic & Conversion Optimization

Data-driven insights for smarter marketing decisions

📌Project Overview

This project analyzes website traffic patterns for an online retail business to improve marketing timing, channel performance, and conversion outcomes. Using Excel, the analysis explores how user engagement varies by time of day, day of the week, and marketing channels such as organic search, paid ads, social media, and email campaigns.
