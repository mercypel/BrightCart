BrightCart Analytics

Traffic and Conversion Optimization Project

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes website traffic patterns for an online retail business to improve marketing timing, channel performance, and conversion outcomes. The analysis explores how user engagement varies by time of day, day of the week, and marketing channels such as organic search, paid ads, social media, and email campaigns.

📊 Dashboard Preview

Excel dashboard showing hourly traffic trends and channel performance.

📈 Key Metrics

  • Number of Sessions: 1,079
  • Distinct Users: 500
  • Conversion Rate: 41.43 percent
  • Number of Conversions: 447
  • Average Session Duration: 3.15 hours
  • Countries Analyzed: US, UK, Canada

🧹 Data Cleaning and Feature Engineering

  • Fixed gender inconsistencies (F and M)
  • Standardized country names (US, UK, Canada)
  • Corrected timestamps to M/D/Y format
  • Removed 103 duplicate records
  • Added engagement data using XLOOKUP
  • Extracted new features: Month, Year, Day, Day Type, Customer Type, Hour, Day Time

🔍 Guiding Questions

  • What day type has more traffic?

💡 Insights Summary

  • Weekdays account for 84.6 percent of all sessions.
  • Canada shows strong morning traffic but lower conversion performance.
  • Social media traffic yields strong conversion rates at 45 percent.
  • Tablet users have the lowest conversion rate at 37 percent.
  • Average session duration dropped by 13 percent, indicating engagement issues.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

  • Prioritize US market with a conversion rate of 47 percent.
  • Redesign strategies for Canada to improve conversion performance.
  • Focus on Organic Search and Direct as primary traffic channels.
  • Expand social media campaigns due to strong conversion results.
  • Enhance page design and content flow to improve engagement.
  • Optimize tablet experience to increase conversion rates.
  • Schedule content releases during peak weekday hours.
